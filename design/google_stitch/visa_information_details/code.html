Long-term Residency

F-2 Residence

expand_more

The F-2 visa is a resident status granted to individuals who wish to stay in Korea for a long term under specific point-based systems or family ties.

Requirements

  • fiber_manual_record Minimum score of 80 points in the evaluation system
  • fiber_manual_record Proof of annual income (GNI x1.0 or higher)
  • fiber_manual_record KIIP (Korea Immigration and Integration Program) certificate
Ethnic Korean

F-4 Overseas Korean

expand_more

The F-4 visa is a quasi-permanent residence status for foreign nationals of Korean descent who previously held Korean citizenship.

Requirements

  • fiber_manual_record Family relationship certificate (Jejok Deungbon)
  • fiber_manual_record Criminal record clearance from country of origin
Permanent

F-5 Permanent Residence

expand_more

The most privileged visa status allowing indefinite stay without renewal restrictions.

Requirements

  • fiber_manual_record Consecutive stay of over 5 years in Korea
Professional Employment

E-7 Special Ability

expand_more

Work visa for specific job categories requiring high-level technical or professional knowledge.

Requirements

  • fiber_manual_record Master's degree or Bachelor's with 1 year experience
Interim Status

D-10 Job Seeking

expand_more

A temporary stay status for graduates looking for professional employment in Korea.

Requirements

  • fiber_manual_record Points system qualification (60+ points total)
Academic

D-2 Study

expand_more

For students enrolled in regular educational programs (undergraduate or higher).

Requirements
